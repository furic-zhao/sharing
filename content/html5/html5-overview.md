### HTML4 vs. XHTML

```markup
<div CLASS=test>This is a <br> test.</div>
<script type="text/javascript">
    alert(1);
</script>
```
```markup
<div class="test">This is a <br/> test.</div>
<script type="text/javascript">
<![CDATA[
    alert(1);
]]>
</script>
```
